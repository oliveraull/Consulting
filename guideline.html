Platforms like TikTok, Reels, and Shorts command billions of daily views. Brands that master short‑form build awareness faster, educate smarter, and convert more customers.

Phase 1
Foundation & Strategy

Define Goals

Brand Awareness • Lead Gen • Customer Education • Community

Know Your Audience

Personas: demographics, pain points, habits, platforms, peak times.

Competitive Analysis

Engaging formats • Frequency • Hashtags • Comment patterns • Content gaps.

Phase 2
Develop Your Content Pillars
Educational — 45%
Entertaining — 30%
Emotional — 25%

Educational: Teach your audience something useful. This builds trust and positions you as an expert. Example: A personal trainer shows proper squat form.

Entertaining: Use humor, trends, or engaging storytelling to grab attention. Example: A baker shows a "day in the life" of baking 100 cupcakes.

Emotional: Share your story, customer success stories, or the "why" behind your business. Example: A small business owner shares their product launch journey.

Phase 3
Master the Basics

Essential Equipment (You Don't Need Fancy)

  • Smartphone: Your phone is enough!
  • High‑Quality Audio: Basic external mic or quiet space
  • Good Lighting: Natural light near window or ring light
  • Tripod/mount: For stability
  • Editing app: CapCut, InShot, Premiere Rush

Hook → Value → CTA

Strong Hook

Question, surprising statement, quick visual to stop the scroll in 3 sec.

Value (Core)

Deliver concisely; use on‑screen text overlays.

CTA (Last 10%)

Specific next step; link/contact.

Key Tips: Most people watch with sound off - use text overlays. BUT! People will forgive shaky video but not bad sound.

Phase 4
Know Your Platform

TikTok

Gen ZDiscovery engineEntertainment
  • Skews younger. Algorithm prioritizes user interests over followers
  • Built for entertainment, trends, and rapid virality

Instagram Reels

Broader audienceEngagement focus
  • Algorithm focuses on engagement with existing followers
  • Blend of entertainment and polished content

YouTube Shorts

Wide age rangeTraffic driver
  • Part of larger YouTube ecosystem
  • Excellent for driving traffic to long‑form videos

Don't post randomly: Each platform has different audiences and algorithms. Tailor your content accordingly.
